# Testing method changed: Mastery Check replaces teach-back-as-gate

The learner found "write a blog paragraph" too fuzzy as a test and asked for a clear
pass/fail mechanism grounded in how the mind actually learns a new topic. They also asked
that math not be front-loaded but introduced at the right moment and explained thoroughly.

Decision (supersedes the teach-back-as-test note in LR-0001): the gate is a three-rung
**Mastery Check** — Recall → Apply → Explain/Transfer — where passing requires clearing
Apply + Explain (application and transfer detect true understanding; recall can be rote).
Fail → targeted re-teach of the missed rung + fresh-variant re-test. The blog paragraph is
demoted to a per-phase *deliverable* (the mission output), not the gate. Math is taught
just-in-time with every symbol explained; the premature 4-equation dump was removed from
Lesson 1. MISSION.md, NOTES.md, and CURRICULUM.md updated accordingly.
